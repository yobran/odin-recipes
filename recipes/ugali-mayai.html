Ugali Mayai

Descrption

Ugali Mayai is a simple and delicious Kenyan meal.

It combines soft ugali with fried eggs for a quick and hearty dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup maize flour (cornmeal)
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. In a pot, bring the water to a boil.
  2. Add a pinch of salt to the boiling water.
  3. Gradually add the maize flour while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  4. Cook the mixture on low heat, stirring until it thickens and pulls away from the sides of the pot. This is your ugali.
  5. In a separate pan, heat oil and fry the eggs to your liking.
  6. Serve the ugali hot with the fried eggs on the side.
